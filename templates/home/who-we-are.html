    Who We Are Page

    We're Gulf Bank , and we're changing the savings game.

    We’re committed to helping you reach your savings goals by offering more rewarding ways to save. Your Gulf Bank Mileage Savings Account earns American Airlines AAdvantage® miles, while your Gulf Bank Interest Savings Account earns 4.03% annual percentage yield (APY).

    Your money is safe with us

    Security and trust matter, which is why we're dedicated to protecting your assets.

    Backed by two decades of experience.

    We launched the first online-only savings bank in the U.S. through Texas Capital Bank in 1999 and have been rewarding people for saving ever since.

    Member FDIC

    As a member of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC), your total deposits are insured up to $250,000 per depositor.

    20 Years with American Airlines.

    We’re taking our 20-year relationship with American Airlines to the next level, offering AAdvantage® miles that grow the more you save.

    We believe that saving for the future shouldn't get in the way of today's adventures.

    In 1999, our founders at Texas Capital Bank pioneered a digital banking experience for their customers. Today, we’re leveraging that experience to provide a more rewarding savings account.

    At Gulf Bank , we set out to challenge the mileage program status quo by rewarding customers for saving, instead of spending.

    Together with American Airlines, we’re proud to offer customers the Gulf Bank Mileage Savings Account — the only savings that earns AAdvantage® miles for every dollar saved.

    We recently launched the Gulf Bank Interest Savings Account to help your savings grow and take you even farther.

    Special Offer

    Deposit $5,000, earn 1,000 AAdvantage<sup>®</sup> miles

    To qualify for this offer, you must be a first time Gulf Bank Mileage Savings Account customer, fund your Gulf Bank Mileage Savings Account with a minimum of $5,000 within 15 calendar days following the initial account opening and maintain a minimum daily account balance of $5,000 for 90 consecutive days out of the first 120 days following the initial account opening.

    AAdvantage<sup>®</sup> bonus miles are awarded within 10 business days upon meeting offer qualifications and may take 6 to 8 weeks to post to your AAdvantage<sup>®</sup> account. The value of this bonus offer will be reported to the IRS, and the recipient is responsible for any federal, state or local taxes on this offer.
